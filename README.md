## How to Use

1. **Open the Application**
   - Open `index.html` in your web browser
   - The page will load with the Thindham restaurant homepage

2. **Navigate the Site**
   - Use the navigation bar to jump between sections
   - Click "Order Now" to go directly to the menu
   - Click "Learn More" to read about the restaurant

3. **Browse the Menu**
   - View all items or filter by category (All, Appetizers, Main Course, Desserts, Beverages)
   - Each item shows name, description, price, and food emoji
   - Click "Add to Cart" to add items to your order

4. **Manage Your Cart**
   - Cart icon shows current item count
   - Click on Cart in navigation to view your order
   - Use +/- buttons to adjust quantities
   - Use trash icon to remove items completely
   - View live calculation of subtotal, tax, delivery, and total

5. **Complete Your Order**
   - Click "Proceed to Checkout" when ready
   - Review your order in the confirmation modal
   - See final total amount
   - Order is confirmed and cart is cleared
